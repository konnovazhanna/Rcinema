How does it work?

The Private Cinema can be set up anywhere you like, be it poolside, on the lawn or even the roof terrace, provided there is an available power outlet. All films must start after sunset. Our team of experts arrive in a fully equipped van and will advise you on the best place to setup the cinema based on our experience.
